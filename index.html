E-signature

WOLAS Revolution allows for you to integrate with third party providers such as Docusign who facilitate paperless signatures.

Docusign allows customers to sign documents electronically either on the computer or directly on their smartphone. This is a great way to get document signed back quickly. If you are aiming to move into a paperless environment (and we think everyone should), e-signature is a massive step that direction.
